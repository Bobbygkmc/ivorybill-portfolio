Restaurant Analytics & Operations Support

A business-focused case study centered on restaurant operations, reporting, workflow visibility, and practical technology support.

Back to Projects

Project Overview

This project reflects my interest in using technology to improve restaurant operations through better reporting, more organized processes, and stronger visibility into performance. The focus was on identifying areas where structured information and practical systems could support better day-to-day decision-making.

The work included thinking through operational pain points such as manual processes, inconsistent visibility, document handling, and the need for more organized workflows. The goal was to support a business environment with technology that is useful, reliable, and easy to maintain.

What This Project Demonstrates

This case study demonstrates my ability to connect business needs with practical technology solutions. It shows how I approach data, workflow improvement, systems thinking, and operational support with a focus on real use rather than theory alone.

Key Focus Areas

Reporting and visibility into operations, structured process improvement, support for business systems, and practical use of technology to reduce friction in daily work.
