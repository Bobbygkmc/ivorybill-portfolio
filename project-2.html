Business Website & Web Design Projects

A case study focused on designing professional, usable, and business-oriented web experiences for small organizations.

Back to Projects

Project Overview

This work focused on building and refining websites that present businesses clearly, support credibility, and improve the user experience. The goal was not just to make a site look good, but to make it useful, structured, and aligned with business needs.

I approached web design from a practical perspective: clean layouts, stronger content structure, easier navigation, and a more professional online presence. This kind of work is especially important for small businesses that need websites to support visibility, trust, and growth.

What This Project Demonstrates

This case study demonstrates my ability to combine design thinking, business communication, and practical implementation. It reflects how I use websites as tools for business support rather than as isolated design pieces.

Key Focus Areas

Web structure, usability, branding, content presentation, business-facing communication, and improving the digital presence of small organizations.
